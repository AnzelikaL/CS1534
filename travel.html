Travel Information for Riga

The capital is served by Riga Airport, offering both direct and connecting flights from various destinations around the world. For those seeking a more eco-friendly option, traveling by train is a great choice, with excellent connections to most EU countries. Alternatively, if you're the adventurous type, Riga is also accessible by car from many neighboring countries with ease.

Getting Around-Riga offers a comprehensive range of transport options, including buses, trains, minibuses, and taxis, ensuring convenient connections throughout the city. The services run frequently, providing easy access to all parts of Riga, and there is typically ample space for passengers. The staff on most services are friendly and helpful, making your journey more pleasant. Payments can be made via card, though cash is also accepted. Additionally, taxis are readily available, offering a comfortable and efficient way to get around the city, whether for a quick trip or a longer journey. Riga's well-developed transport network makes it easy to explore the city and beyond.
