Visitor Information for Riga

Experience Riga

Riga offers a rich cultural experience with its history, architecture, and vibrant atmosphere.

  • Explore the cobbled streets of the Old Town.
  • Admire the Art Nouveau buildings.
  • Shop and taste local produce at the Riga Central Market.
  • Enjoy a night at the Latvian National Opera.
  • Visit the iconic St. Peter's Church for panoramic views.
